Brief Introduction of Discipline

School of Electronic Science and Engineering, which was one of China's earliest disciplines concerning electronics, was established in Nov. 1961. School of Electronic Science and Engineering has the key disciplines of national “Project 211" and “Program 985", and is the key school of Southeast University(SEU) that integrates teaching, scientific research and technology development . The Electronic Science&Technology discipline of Southeast University is National first-level key discipline, it ranks 2nd in National Discipline Assessment of the CDGDC of Ministry of Education in 2012. The discipline consists of 3 second-level key discipline: 1. Physical Electronics (National key discipline);   2. Microelectronics & Solid State Electronics (National key discipline);   3. Circuits and Systems (National key discipline).

Training Objectives

The objectives are to provide a joint training program based on both side advantages in Electronic Science&Technology area. Graduates will have the knowledge and skills to engage in scientific researc or ,technological development. Students will develop deep theoretical and practical knowledge in specific areas so that they will have the intellectual and conceptual foundation to play leading roles in theElectronic Science&Technology industry. Generally, the students are expected to graduate in 2 or 2.5 years.

Research Direction

1 Display Science and Technology
2 Optoelectronic Engineering and Photo-communication Technology
3 Optical Communication/Sensing and Networking Technology
4 Embedded System
5 Integrated Circuit and System Design
6 Micro-Electro-Mechanical System(MEMS/NEMS)

Thesis Requirement

(1) Literature Reading (for reference only, the same below)
Students are required to read at least 30 literatures on topics related to their thesis and research direction and to write a literature overview.
(2) Thesis Writing
In addition to compliance with the provisions of school, thesis must be written in a systematic and complete manner, presenting clear concepts, correct argument, realistic description, concise writing structure, smooth and concise writing style, correct calculation, reliable data, and clear icons. The number of words for the thesis could be fixed according to the features of different disciplines and the requirements of the topics chosen, generally, no more than 20,000 words.
(3) Thesis Evaluation 
The thesis should be written based on students’ own study and research. The findings presented in the thesis should have high academic value and positive impact on economic development of China. Students are required to conduct at least one year research concerning the thesis topic.
(4) Achievement Test Requirement
At least 1 papers should be accepted or published, or 1 invention patent should be submitted before applying for a Master’s Degree.
